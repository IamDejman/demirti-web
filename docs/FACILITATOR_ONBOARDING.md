n# CVERSE LMS Facilitator Onboarding

## Access
- Log in at `/login` with your facilitator credentials.
- You will land on `/facilitator`.

## Daily flow
1. Review your assigned cohorts.
2. Check the **Grading** queue for new submissions.
3. Post announcements or reminders (via admin if needed).
4. Monitor attendance and recordings after live classes.

## Teaching workflow
- Weeks and content are managed in the admin cohort UI.
- Assignments appear automatically in student dashboards once published.
- Provide feedback through the grading queue.

## Communication
- Use cohort chat and DMs for learner support.
- Report any abusive or off-topic content; admins will review via moderation.

## Tips
- Keep a consistent cadence for weekly updates.
- Use attendance and grading data to identify at-risk students.
